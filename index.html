About Me

Development

My name is Till, and I am an enthusiastic programmer and Discord user.
Since the age of 16, I have been heavily involved in the development of Discord bots, and recently, I released my own bot, Airbot, in beta phase.
I largely taught myself programming skills.
I spent hours reading tutorials, studying source code, and interacting with other developers to improve my skills. Currently, my bot is in beta phase, and I am working hard to continually improve it and add new features. I am proud of what I have accomplished so far, and I look forward to further expanding my skills as a developer and making my bot even better. My goal is to develop a bot that provides real value to Discord users and enhances the Discord experience. I believe that with the right approach and work ethic, anything is possible, and I am determined to do my best to achieve my goals.
